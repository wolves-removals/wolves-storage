Managed Storage in Brighton & Hove — We Come to You

Storing in Brighton & Hove the traditional way is a nightmare — narrow Victorian terraces, controlled parking, communal stairwells and very few affordable units. The managed model solves it: we bring the team and the vehicle to your BN1, BN2 or BN3 door, pack and wrap everything, and take it away. No van hire, no lifting, no driving across the city. See how it works.

Your belongings are sealed into your own wooden container and stored at our alarmed warehouse near the A27, then redelivered whenever you’re ready. It’s fully managed container storage from just £15 a week, with collection and redelivery included.

Storage for Brighton’s Flats, Students & Downsizers

With two universities and a huge rental market, Brighton & Hove is full of people who need short bursts of storage — students storing over summer, tenants between lets, and couples merging flats. Our weekly short-term storage is billed by the week with no long contract, and we collect from halls, HMOs and flats alike.

We also help Hove’s downsizers and families moving out of the city with long-term storage and careful furniture storage. Whatever the timescale, it’s fully insured and from £15 a week.
